About Me

Hi there! I'm Taige, a designer and developer with a passion for creating beautiful, functional digital experiences. I've been working in the tech industry for over 5 years, specializing in frontend development and UI/UX design.

My journey started when I was studying Computer Science and discovered my love for the intersection of technology and creativity. Since then, I've worked with startups, agencies, and established companies to bring their digital products to life.

When I'm not coding or designing, you can find me exploring nature with my camera, experimenting with new creative techniques, or diving into a good book. I'm also an avid traveler and enjoy capturing moments and inspiration from different cultures and landscapes.

Through this blog, I aim to share my knowledge, experiences, and creative explorations with others who share similar interests. Whether you're a fellow designer, developer, or just curious about the digital world, I hope you'll find something valuable here.
